Why Aged Care Flooring Matters in Brisbane

Queensland's aged care sector is expanding faster than almost anywhere else in Australia. Population growth, the retirement migration to South-East Queensland, and increasing regulatory pressure mean more facilities are being built, upgraded, and audited than ever before. If you're managing a facility, you're dealing with compliance demands, risk management, and the practical reality of keeping operations running during upgrades.

Brisbane's subtropical climate adds another layer. Humidity affects adhesive performance, moisture migration through slabs is more common, and wet weather tracking creates slip risks that temperate-climate facilities don't face as often. Your flooring needs to account for these conditions — not just meet a standard on paper, but perform in the actual environment.

That's what we solve. We specify flooring that meets AS 4586 slip resistance standards and performs in Queensland conditions. We work around your operational schedule — including after-hours and staged installations. We provide the testing and documentation auditors want to see. And we've done enough projects across Brisbane to understand the real constraints of your building, your climate, and your team.
