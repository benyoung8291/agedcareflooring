Frequently Asked Questions

Do you service regional Victoria?

We're based in Melbourne, so inner and outer suburbs are our bread and butter. For regional Victoria — Bendigo, Ballarat, Geelong and beyond — we can do it, but we need projects that justify the drive. Call and tell us where you are. If it makes sense, we'll do it.

What about Sydney or Brisbane?

We've got local teams in both cities. Same service model, local knowledge, local inventory. Get in touch to connect with the right team.

How long does a typical installation take?

Depends on the scope. A bedroom refurb? Few days. A whole facility? Weeks, usually staged around your schedule. That's why we do after-hours work — you're still running the facility while we're installing. We'll give you a realistic timeline when we visit.

What if there's asbestos in the existing floor?

We don't remove asbestos — that's a licensed remediation job. But once it's removed and cleared, we install your new flooring. We can coordinate with the removal contractor to keep things moving.
