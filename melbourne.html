Why Aged Care Flooring Matters in Melbourne

Melbourne's aged care sector is expanding. More facilities opening, more operators upgrading older buildings, more regulatory pressure to demonstrate safety compliance. If you're managing a facility, you're probably getting pressure on multiple fronts: accreditation audits, litigation risk, staffing costs, resident quality of life.

Here's what the industry actually cares about: Can your flooring prevent slips and falls? Does it reduce infection transmission? Does it help residents with dementia navigate the space without confusion or distress? Can you document compliance for auditors? And critically — can you install it without shutting down your facility?

That's what we solve. We specify flooring that meets AS 4586 slip resistance standards. We work with your operational schedule. We provide the testing and documentation auditors want to see. We've done enough aged care projects in Melbourne to know the actual constraints of your building, your residents, and your staff.
