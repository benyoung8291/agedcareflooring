The Problem NSW Aged Care Facilities Face

Aged care facilities across Sydney and NSW are under pressure. Slip and fall incidents create litigation risk. Accreditation audits demand documented compliance. Worn-out flooring harbours bacteria and creates infection control problems. And replacing floors in a live facility, without relocating residents or shutting down wings, is a logistical headache most contractors don't understand.

Sydney's aged care sector is one of the largest in Australia. From heritage-listed buildings in the Inner West to purpose-built facilities in Western Sydney, each project comes with its own set of constraints. The flooring needs to be slip-resistant, infection-resistant, dementia-appropriate, and compliant, and it needs to be installed without disrupting the people who live there.

That's exactly what Premrest solves. We supply and install flooring specifically for aged care: Interface, Shaw Contract, Polyflor, and Forbo products that meet AS 4586 slip resistance standards. We work around your facility's schedule, including after-hours and staged installations. And we provide the testing and documentation auditors want to see.

Why Aged Care Flooring Matters in Sydney

Sydney has the highest concentration of aged care facilities in Australia. With an ageing population and increasing regulatory scrutiny, facility operators face mounting pressure to demonstrate compliance, reduce risk, and improve resident outcomes. The flooring under your residents' feet is one of the most direct ways to address all three.

The real questions are practical: Can your flooring prevent slips and falls in wet areas? Does it reduce infection transmission? Does it help residents with dementia navigate without confusion? Can you document compliance for the Aged Care Quality and Safety Commission? And can you get it installed without relocating residents or shutting down wings?

That's what we solve. We specify flooring that meets AS 4586 slip resistance standards. We work around your operational schedule, including after-hours and staged installations. We provide the testing and documentation auditors want to see. And we've done enough projects across Sydney to understand the real constraints of your building, your residents, and your team.
